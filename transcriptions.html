Of Adano Ley Satsangs

Current Transcription

September 7, 2023

(continued from last post)

Now the chemist has left a chemical agent for Man to find out what his mechanism is to a certain extent and to give him a certain type of peace, a certain type of relief, and a possible type of expansion of awareness, but it did not say it gave the man permanent peace, it didn’t say it gave the man intuitive development, it didn’t say it gave the man wisdom, it didn’t say it gave the man cosmic consciousness. Now here is a typical example of one of the tests that the Sages put forward of a person who has claimed that they experience some form of expanded consciousness or awareness by the chemical agent versus what they themselves have achieved through their own meditative process. There was a man who applied a chemical agent and had a tremendous extension of awareness and came to a spiritual teacher and said “I have achieved cosmic consciousness” and the teacher looked at him and said “Well I don’t think you have, I think you have found the whole topography or the whole geography of your subconscious mind for the first time and actually saw how it functioned.” “Oh no I have had cosmic consciousness, I have felt the presence of God.” The Sage looked at him and says, “Alright, if you have, we are here in America and I was born in India, there’s a city called Calcutta, on Road Andrew Street is the name of the street, room 14 which is a store, there is an Indian man wearing a turban right there at this moment, he’s selling sweetmeats, I want you now, right in your body here, don’t go out of this room, send you cosmic consciousness to that man and let him give you one of those sweetmeats and transport it back into this room and let us eat it.”

— Adano Ley (Guru Disciple Relationship - November 1968 - 0:48 - Audio on YouTube - All Adano Transcriptions)
