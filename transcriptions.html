Of Adano Ley Satsangs

Current Transcription

May 27, 2023

(continued from last post)

There’s no car coming down this road with ice and if it does they blow their horn or they have a bell and he told his mother he says “Mumsy, look at this ice.” “Well George it’s not ours because we didn’t order it and the ice man doesn’t come around today.” So he says, “Well if nobody don’t pick it up in the next hour, I’m gonna use it.” Well you can imagine a 50 pound ice in the blazing sun in Montreal Canada about 112 degrees, how long can 50 pound ice last? It will be all melted out right? An hour past and that ice was still there, nobody claimed it so he walked down and he went to that ice and lifted it up. No sooner than he lifted it up, you would think there would be a puddle of water, right? There was not a single puddle of water, just a square outline where the ice rested and it was like a distinct line like if someone took a crayon and just marked the outline of the ice right there. He picked that ice up and started up the stairway. Well we ran down to help him, to our surprise it was like the ice was lifting him up the stairway, bringing him up to the top. Well I’ll tell you, I drank the rum and coke, but there was no actual flavor in that rum and coke no matter how much rum I put in that glass, the ice and everything, couldn’t get the flavor of the rum. I felt I was cheated after drinking the rum and coke because couldn’t taste the flavor of the rum in the rum and coke. Well George says to me, “Man this is too serious” because he didn’t expect something like this to happen.

— Adano Ley (Guru Disciple Relationship - November 1968 - 0:16 - Audio on YouTube - All Adano Transcriptions)
